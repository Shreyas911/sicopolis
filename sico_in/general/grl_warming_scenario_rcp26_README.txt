--------------------------------------------------
Simplified RCP2.6 warming scenarios for Greenland,
devised by Rueckamp et al. (2019)
--------------------------------------------------

* grl_warming_scenario_rcp26.asc

  Simplified RCP2.6 scenario based on results from three general
  circulation models (GCMs) within the Inter-Sectoral Impact Model
  Intercomparison Project (ISIMIP2b; Frieler et al., 2017).

* grl_warming_scenario_rcp26woo.asc

  Like above, but the interim overshoot has been eliminated.
